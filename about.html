OUR STORY

We build teams, but more importantly, we forge champions. ROSO Esports finds raw talent and helps turn it into competitive excellence across the games that matter most.

The name ROSO comes from Rosoideae, a family of plants known for being resilient and beautiful. Like them, we believe in creating the right environment for talent to grow. Every player on our roster gets the coaching, resources, and platform they need to succeed.
