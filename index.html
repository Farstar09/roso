WHO WE ARE

ROSO Esports is a premier competitive gaming organization that bridges the gap between STEM excellence and esports performance. Our mission is to cultivate talent, foster innovation, and compete at the highest levels across multiple gaming titles.

Inspired by the Rosoideae family - where beauty meets strength - we nurture our players' growth both in and out of the game, creating an environment where talent truly blooms.
